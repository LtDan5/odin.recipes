Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a dessert with layers consisting of espresso-covered lady fingers and a creamy filling. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Heavy Cream
  • Marscarpone Cheese
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Lady fingers
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Espresso
  • Rum

Steps

  1. Combine egg yolks and half of the sugar to a bowl and whip thoroughly.
  2. Combine egg whites and the other half of the sugar to a bowl and whip until firm peaks are formed.
  3. Mix egg yolks mixture, heavy cream and marscapone cheese. Then slowly add in the egg whites mixture and mix.
  4. Grab a cake dish and dust the bottom of it with half of the cocoa powder.
  5. Add the rum to the espresso mixture.
  6. Dip the lady fingers in the rum-espresso mixture and line the bottom of the dish with half of them.
  7. Spread half of the cream base over the lady fingers.
  8. Add another layer of lady fingers and cream.
  9. Top with a dusting of the remaining cocoa powder.
  10. Cover and place in the fridge for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight.
