Creme Brulee

Description

Creme Brulee is a custard topped with sugar and torched to create a glass-like shell.

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Warm the heavy cream for 5 minutes then remove from stove.
  2. Add the vanilla to heavy cream mixture.
  3. Whip the egg yolks, sugar and salt. Slowly add in the cream.
  4. Fill ramekins with egg cream mixture.
  5. Add ramekins to baking dish that is filled with boiling water.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Remove ramekins from dish and place on a rack to cool.
  7. Cover and refrigerate ramekins for at least 8 hours, but up to 2 days in the refrigerator.
  8. When ready to serve, remove from fridge and sprinkle 1 tbsp of sugar on top of custard.
  9. Using a torch, toast the sugar on top to create a glass-like shell.
