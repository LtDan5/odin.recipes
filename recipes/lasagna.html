Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish with layers of meat sauce and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 375 F degrees
  2. Cook your ground beef and add pasta sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, eggs and parmesan cheese
  4. Cook lasagna sheets and line backing dish with one third of the sheets.
  5. Add one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture to the dish.
  6. Add one-third of the meat sauce to the dish.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until all ingredients are used.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until golden brown.
